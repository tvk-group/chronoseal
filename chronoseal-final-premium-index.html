How It Works

Simple concept. Powerful trust layer.

ChronoSeal follows a clean proof-generation flow: capture, hash, timestamp, seal, and verify.

Step 01

Capture

A document, event, decision, transaction, or action is submitted to the system.

Step 02

Hash

A cryptographic fingerprint is generated without exposing sensitive underlying content.

Step 03

Seal

The hash, timestamp, metadata, and verification context are recorded into a proof layer.

Step 04

Verify

Anyone with permission can later prove integrity, timing, and authenticity of the record.
